UFC on ABC 4: Rising Stars Shine and AI Proves its Worth in Predicting Outcomes

14/05/2023

The Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC) returned to Charlotte, North Carolina, for the fifth time on May 13, 2023. The event, UFC on ABC: Rozenstruik vs. Almeida, took place at the Spectrum Center, marking the promotion's first visit to Charlotte since UFC on Fox: Jacaré vs. Brunson 2 in January 2018.

A light heavyweight bout between former UFC Light Heavyweight Championship challenger Anthony Smith and Johnny Walker was originally expected to headline the event. However, the UFC decided to promote a scheduled three-round heavyweight bout between Jairzinho Rozenstruik and Jailton Almeida to the five-round main event spot, downgrading the initial headliner to a co-main event.

The main event saw Jailton Almeida secure a swift and decisive victory over Jairzinho Rozenstruik, submitting him with a rear-naked choke in the first round. The co-main event had Johnny Walker defeating Anthony Smith via unanimous decision after three intense rounds of combat.

One notable performance on the main card was from rising star Ian Machado Garry. His fight against Daniel Rodriguez ended with a spectacular TKO finish due to a head kick and punches in the first round. Garry's impressive performance solidified his status as one to watch in the welterweight division.

In a welterweight bout, Bryan Battle replaced the injured Jake Matthews and faced Gabriel Green. Despite weighing in two pounds over the limit, the bout proceeded at catchweight, and Battle scored a stunning KO victory in just 14 seconds.

On the women's side, a flyweight bout between Kim Ji-yeon and Mandy Böhm resulted in a technical decision in favor of Böhm due to multiple infractions from Kim.

The event also saw a bout scrapped due to weight management issues from Pete Rodriguez, who was set to face Natan Levy in a lightweight match.

As for our AI Fight Predictor, it demonstrated impressive accuracy, going 5/5 on the main card and 4/6 on the prelims. This level of precision highlights the potential of AI technology in predicting fight outcomes and offers exciting possibilities for fans and bettors alike.

Overall, UFC on ABC: Rozenstruik vs. Almeida was an event marked by impressive finishes, a bit of controversy, and a showcase of rising stars in the sport. Fans and fighters alike now eagerly anticipate the next event, UFC Fight Night: Dern vs. Hill, headlined by a women's strawweight bout between Mackenzie Dern and former Invicta FC Strawweight Champion Angela Hill.
